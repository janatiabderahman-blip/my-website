Digital Payments & Holds

Payment platforms like PayPal, Wise, and Cash App often place temporary holds for security or verification reasons. Understanding why funds are held and what steps you can take calmly is key to resolving issues without panic.

  • Review official notifications inside your account dashboard.
  • Avoid sending multiple disputes at once—follow one clear path.
  • Keep communication polite and factual when contacting support.
